about me

I am 27 years old and am a devoted resident of San Francisco! I currently work at a health network called Sutter Health. I am just finishing a year-long rotational management program where--in addition to various projects--I oversaw a Radiology Department with 28 employees. It was a wonderful experience. My first full-time career was in management consulting with the Boston Consulting Group, where I worked for 3 years in various locations including Los Angeles, San Francisco, New York, Brussels, Zurich, London, and Rotterdam.

I also have had the opportunity to learn from some incredible academic institutions. Prior to joining Sutter, I received my MBA from the Stanford Graduate School of Business. While there, I focused on healthcare, specifically healthcare innovation. I had the opportunity to learn from some of the brightest minds and business leaders in the world and met some incredibly interesting people. I received my undergraduate degree from Yale in Economics and Math. My senior project was a proof of the Bourgain Embedding Theorem!

I am an avid cook and foodie! I love all things that sizzle, smell, and pop. I love experimenting and trying new things - I rarely cook the same recipe twice. My most recent adventures were a Moroccan lamb shank tagine and homemade wonton noodle soup with braised pork. I am already getting hungry.
